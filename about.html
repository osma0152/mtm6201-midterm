About Us

Flour Power is Ottawa’s premier cookie shop, offering a wide variety of meticulously crafted cookies that are sure to delight. With a commitment to quality and innovation, they infuse every bite with love and creativity, creating flavors ranging from classic chocolate chip to unique combinations like salted caramel and lavender. Whether you’re in need of a sweet treat, a thoughtful gift, or catering for a special event, Flour Power has the perfect cookies to elevate any occasion. Visit their website to explore their selection and experience the joy of their delicious creations in Ottawa, Ontario.
